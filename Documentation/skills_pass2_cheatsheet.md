# Shards Idle — Skills Pass 2 Cheatsheet
60 new skills. Format: Parent + Parent = Child — rationale.

---

## Cross-Domain Damage

**slash + shadow_bolt = Shadow Wound** — A cutting strike leaves a shadow-infused wound that keeps bleeding in another dimension. Slashing + lingering shadow debuff.

**pummel + arcane_bolt = Runic Smash** — Runes are carved into the strike mid-impact. Weight of bludgeoning meets volatile arcane force.

**shock + apply_poison = Neuro Toxin** — Electric current delivers venom directly through the nervous system. Stuns and poisons simultaneously.

**apply_poison + arcane_bolt = Volatile Venom** — Arcane energy destabilises toxins into an explosive alchemical bolt. Longer poison duration than normal.

**chill + holy_light = Divine Frost** — Holy purity expressed as absolute stillness and cold. Two things that "stop" — freezing and sanctifying.

**produce_flame + apply_poison = Septic Flame** — Fire that carries infection. Burns and poisons together; the wound doesn't heal clean.

**shadow_bolt + nature_touch = Entropic Decay** — Shadow as natural entropy, not supernatural evil. Rot is what nature does to everything eventually.

**shock + nature_touch = Storm Seed** — Lightning planted like a seed into flesh, growing into a charge that keeps discharging.

**chill + blood_letting = Blood Ice** — Cold applied to an open wound freezes the blood internally. Compounds bleed with cold damage.

**shove + produce_flame = Flaming Shove** — Push someone into a burst of fire. Simple, brutal, physical + fire.

**shove + shock = Shockwave** — Electric shove radiates outward as a concussive wave. AOE knockback with electric damage.

**shove + holy_light = Holy Shove** — Repel evil with divine force. Knockback with holy damage, thematically clean.

**pierce + nature_touch = Nature Pierce** — Leaves thorns in the wound. Piercing attack that causes a bleed from natural barbs.

**lunge + apply_poison = Poison Lunge** — Deliver venom mid-charge directly into the target. Fast poison application.

**lunge + shadow_step = Phantom Lunge** — Disappear mid-charge, reappear at the target. Untargetable during the gap.

**slash + footwork = Wind Cut** — Footwork creates a vacuum that extends slash range. Speed amplifying a blade's reach.

**basic_attack + attunement = Grounded Strike** — Draw power from the earth itself into a basic blow. Stability translating to force.

**pummel + arcane_strike = Arcane Pummel** — Arcane strike energy loaded into a heavy blow. Straightforward hybrid, higher ceiling than either parent.

**shield_bash + arcane_bolt = Arcane Shield Bash** — Arcane energy detonates on contact with the shield slam. Physical/magic hybrid burst.

## AOE Damage

**smothering_darkness** (toxic_fog + shadow_veil) — Stealth poison cloud that can't be seen or avoided. The two concealment schools merging into a lethal field.

**plague_bloom** (plague_carrier + regrowth) — Twisted regrowth spreads plague instead of health. Nature's growth applied to disease.

**venom_storm** (poison_cloud + lightning_chain) — Lightning arcing through a poison cloud carries toxic spores to every target it jumps to.

**decay_aura** (shadow_bolt + attunement) — Shadow attuned to natural cycles becomes a persistent entropy field around the caster.

**shockwave** (shove + shock) — See above, listed here too as it's AOE.

## Control

**sacred_roots** (entangle + holy_light) — Holy light causes the roots to grow with divine purpose. Restrains and damages with holy energy.

**holy_roots** (smite + entangle) — Vines empowered by smite energy. Divine conviction physically manifesting as restraint.

**rotting_grasp** (shadow_grasp + nature_touch) — Shadow tendrils infused with natural decay. Grasps and rots simultaneously.

**frozen_cry** (warcry + frost_nova) — A shout so forceful it drops the ambient temperature. The sound wave becomes a cold wave.

**judgment_field** (holy_word + entangle) — A holy command that manifests as entangling vines to hold enemies for judgment.

---

## Defense

**faith_armor** (stone_skin + prayer) — Divine will hardening the body. Prayer making the stone skin permanent and sacred.

**divine_barrier** (mana_shield + prayer) — Mana shield elevated by prayer into a spiritual wall. Higher protection, longer duration.

**bark_carapace** (nature_wrap + stone_skin) — Living armor of bark and stone. Nature and earth combining into layered physical protection.

**crystal_skin** (arcane_bolt + stone_skin) — Arcane energy crystallises the stone into magical glass. Physical armor with spell resistance.

**shadow_block** (block + shadow_veil) — A block that phases partially into shadow to reduce how much of the hit lands. Evasive defense.

**ice_block** (block + chill) — A block that freezes the attacker's weapon or fist on contact. Defensive and slows the attacker.

**fire_wall** (produce_flame + block) — A block that projects a wall of flame forward. Damages anyone trying to close in.

**sanctuary** (holy_word + block) — A divine command creates a protected zone. Holy word defining the space, block defending it.

**divine_reflection** (spell_reflection + holy_light) — Spell reflection elevated by holy light — reflects AND heals or protects on success.

## Healing

**sacred_grove** (holy_light + nature_touch) — Blessed natural growth field. The two most life-affirming schools combining into a sustained AOE heal.

**thorned_regeneration** (regrowth + nature_wrap) — Living armor that heals while vines punish attackers. Defensive healing with a cost for enemies.

**caustic_mend** (mend + poison_cloud) — Medicinal herbs are toxic to the wrong organism. A healing mist that restores allies and damages enemies in the same field.

**martyrdom** (healing_light + basic_attack) — Sacrifice your own health to fuel a powerful AOE heal. The physical cost of basic attack channelled into healing radiance.

**retaliatory_heal** (mend + counter_strike) — Healing triggered by successfully countering. The momentum of the counter feeds the mend.

**persistent_life** (regrowth + rest) — Rest deepens regrowth into a multi-turn HoT. Longer, slower, more thorough recovery.

**resurrection_rite** (holy_word + first_aid) — Divine command combined with medical knowledge to revive fallen allies. The word that restarts the body.

**life_link** (holy_light + attunement) — Attunement allows holy light to resonate between targets, sharing the healing across party members.

**alchemy_fire** (produce_flame + first_aid) — Cauterisation. Fire carefully applied to wounds to seal them. Heals the user while dealing fire damage to the enemy.

**atonement** (mend + prayer) — Mending through prayerful sacrifice. Physical healing elevated by spiritual devotion.

**penitent_strike** (smite + blood_letting) — A holy strike that heals the caster from the blood spilled. Fighting as penance; violence with a restorative cost.

## Utility / Buff / Restoration

**shadow_misdirection** (shadow_step + misdirect) — Combine vanishing with deception to create multiple false images. Extended stealth and evasion boost.

**mana_cycle** (vitality_boost + arcane_bolt) — Convert stamina recovery into mana generation. Energy cycling between the two pools.

**terror_cry** (warcry + shadow_bolt) — A battle cry infused with shadow energy. Demoralises enemies with fear damage rather than inspiring allies.

**rooted_healing** (healing_light + entangle) — A healing field that roots enemies who enter it. Safety zone: allies recover, enemies are held.

**shadow_riposte** (counter_strike + shadow_step) — After blocking, vanish and reappear behind the attacker. Counter that repositions the caster.

**corrosive_wound** (venomous_slash + acid_splash) — Acid and venom stacking into a wound that never closes. Compounding decay on a single target.

**echoing_prayer** (prayer + shout) — A prayer projected loudly across the battlefield. The shout carries the blessing to all allies at once.

**silent_prayer** (prayer + misdirect) — A prayer cast quietly enough that enemies don't detect the buff. Stealth buffing.

**focused_rest** (rest + focus) — Resting with full mental concentration accelerates recovery. Both parents are about recovering — together they're faster.

**hunter_mark** (aim + stalk) — Stalking reveals weaknesses that aiming alone wouldn't find. Applies a weaken before the strike.

**stalking_shadow** (stalk + shadow_step) — Shadow stepping while stalking. Move silently and unseen across the battlefield.

**spirit_walk** (void_walk + attunement) — Void walk attuned to the natural spirit realm. Longer, safer, more controlled version of void_walk.

---

## Notable Combinations Worth Equipping Together

- **sacred_grove + rooted_healing** — double AOE healing, enemies trapped in your recovery zone
- **neuro_toxin + volatile_venom** — two poison delivery methods from different schools, stacking DoT
- **shadow_misdirection + phantom_lunge** — vanish, then reappear mid-charge; very hard to target
- **caustic_mend + plague_bloom** — healing field + disease bloom; your party recovers while enemies decay
- **faith_armor + crystal_skin** — physical AND magical defense stacked; essentially a tank build
- **terror_cry + frozen_cry** — shadow fear + cold stun; two different crowd control types back to back
